Space Invaders 🐱‍🏍

Welcome!

Your mission is to kill all the aliens before they land on earth!

* Alien kill adds 20 points to the score!👽

* Keys ⬅ / ➡ to move the player left / right

* 'SPACE' key to shoot

* 'X' key to shoot super cat! 🐱‍🏍 (you have only 3 super cats each game)

* 'N' key to super shoot! (you have only 2 super shoot each game)

* If you hit the 🍭 - the aliens freeze for 5 seconds and gives you 50 points to the score!
